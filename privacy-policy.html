Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2025

Overview

PrepAI is an interview preparation app that helps you practice and improve your interview skills. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data PrepAI collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data Storage

All your personal data — including your name, career preferences, interview sessions, flashcards, quiz results, and study progress — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. PrepAI does not upload or sync this data to any external server. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed.

AI Services

PrepAI offers multiple AI providers to power interview practice:


Apple Intelligence: Processes your prompts entirely on-device. No data leaves your device.


Anthropic (Claude) and OpenAI (ChatGPT): If you choose to use a third-party AI provider, your interview conversation text is sent to that provider's API to generate responses. These requests are made directly from your device to the provider. PrepAI does not store, log, or have access to this traffic on any intermediary server. Please review Anthropic's and OpenAI's respective privacy policies for how they handle API data.


API keys you enter for third-party providers are stored securely in your device's Keychain and are never transmitted anywhere other than to the respective AI provider's API.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

PrepAI's Voice Mode uses your device's microphone and Apple's on-device speech recognition to transcribe your spoken answers. Audio is processed locally on your device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted. You can revoke microphone and speech recognition permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

Data Collection

PrepAI does not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, or any personal information. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs. We do not sell, share, or rent your data to anyone.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to use Anthropic or OpenAI as your AI provider, your conversation data is subject to their privacy policies. PrepAI has no control over how these third-party services process data sent to their APIs. Use of third-party providers is entirely optional.

Children's Privacy

PrepAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing.
